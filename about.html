About

A digital millenial publication with focus on Black Muslim Women Empowerment through Activism, Fashion, Beauty and Lifestyle.

In addition to candid discussions about Women in Islam and Social Trends.

The WildSatement Journal represents the women of African decent and in the diaspora who are widely invading and engaging with their inner selves to discover, take control, represent and explore many narratives and perspectives through Activism, Fashion, Beauty, Style and Lifestyle to innovate and transform.

We proceed with all odds to revolutionize, evolve and create the unimaginable worthy of celebration and recognition adjacent to representation.

Fatima N, Founder & Editor-in-Chief, The WildStatement Journal
